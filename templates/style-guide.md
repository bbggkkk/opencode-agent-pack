- Avoid generic filler, flat exposition, and unexplained tonal swings.

- Do not directly imitate living authors; convert references into broad traits.

## Formatting

- Paragraphs are separated by blank lines.
- Narrative paragraphs have no leading spaces or tabs.
- Dialogue begins on a new line and uses double quotes.
