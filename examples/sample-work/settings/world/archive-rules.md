# Archive Rules

- Name: sealed archive witness protocol.
- World Rule: The sealed archive door opens only when two living witnesses speak their legal names.
- Scope: sealed archive door access, witness identity confirmation, and witness voice recognition.
- Limitations: The brass witness key confirms the holder's identity but cannot open the door alone; recorded voices cannot satisfy the witness requirement.
- Exceptions: none established in verified text.
- Evidence Requirements: any successful door opening must show two living witnesses speaking legal names on-page.
- Active State Anchors: opens only when two living witnesses speak legal names; recorded voices cannot satisfy the witness requirement; cannot identify the voice.
- Continuity Risks: letting one key open the door, using a recording as a witness, or allowing a witness to identify an unknown person's voice without established source.
